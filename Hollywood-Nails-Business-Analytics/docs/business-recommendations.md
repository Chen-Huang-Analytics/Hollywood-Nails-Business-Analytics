# Business Recommendations

## 1. Customer retention
Create a 90-day inactive-customer list and prioritize customers with the highest historical lifetime value. Use appointment reminders and targeted re-engagement messaging.

## 2. Staffing alignment
Compare completed appointments by weekday and hour. Schedule more coverage during peak periods and reduce excess coverage during slower periods.

## 3. Service mix
Promote services that combine strong demand with high estimated gross profit. Review low-volume services before adding more inventory or appointment time.

## 4. Booking sources
Measure completion, cancellation, and no-show rates by booking source. Use this analysis to decide where marketing and scheduling effort should be concentrated.

## 5. Inventory control
Review physical-versus-expected inventory variance monthly. Investigate repeated variances for waste, inaccurate recording, or inconsistent product usage.
